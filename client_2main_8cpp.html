Detailed Description

Scalable UDP echo client.

The client creates one or more worker threads per CPU. Each worker affinitizes sockets and threads to a specific logical processor, posts asynchronous receives, and sends UDP packets containing a sequence number and timestamp. Received echoes are used to compute RTT and detect lost packets.
